Who we are

We’re just a humble pair of family restaurants with a simple goal of bringing those delicious meals from the coast back into the central valley. Being away from any tourist traps also gives us the benefit of dishing out our Fish n Chips at a more reasonable price.
So if you ever are in the seafood mood but don’t want to drive hours to the coast, we got your fix covered!

Traditional & Sustainable

Since 2007 we have been hard at work maintaining our traditional cooking methods to keep the food as good as you remembered. While we prepare your meals all the same, we have been advancing in sourcing our ingredients.
With the ongoing climate, we have reached out to local farms of both fish and crops to ensure our business stays afloat. With fresh ingredients weekly, it’s guaranteed that each bite is as high quality as humanly possible!

What Makes Us Special?

Unlike other Californian Fish Grills out there, our establishment prioritizes food quality and taste over fancy aesthetics or single-stall bathrooms.
We wanted to emulate that feel of real coastal restauraunts, the ones that have weathered over time but stood nonetheless. It may be old, but it's still fashionable!
